Frequently asked questions

Quick answers for visitors and search engines (FAQ rich results).

What are AI agents?
AI agents combine LLMs with tools and goals to plan steps — often in multi-agent systems that split roles like research, drafting, and QA.
How is this different from a chatbot?
Agents can call tools, retain task state, and coordinate with other agents; the UX challenge is making that power understandable and safe.
Do I need an account today?
Not for this landing page. When demos go live, we may offer anonymous trials first, then optional registration.
Will there be a subscription?
Yes — planned tiers for higher limits, premium agents, and team seats. Join the waitlist for launch pricing.
